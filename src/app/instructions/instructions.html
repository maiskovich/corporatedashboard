The Corporate Dashboard is a tool for showing CSV files in an interactive way.

  • In the "Employees map" section you can upload your CSV(containing "lat" and "lng" fields) and visualize it on a map.
  • In the "Metrics" section you can visualize your CSV data on a line and bar graph.
  • In the "Spreadsheet" section you visualize, sort and filter your CSV data in a table.

Your CSV files need to have specific fields titles for each section of the Corporate Dashboard.
In each section you can download an example CSV file. Copy the fields titles from the example file and fill your data in.


All the data is being stored in your browser, locally, the privacy of your data is protected.
